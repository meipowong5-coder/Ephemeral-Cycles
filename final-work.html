Ephemeral Cycle
Wong Mei Po
2026
Interactive installation: music box spring mechanism, barrier-grid animation on positive film, found wood, sulfur paper, and directional spotlights.
This work reinterprets memento mori through a manual music box mechanism, inviting viewers to engage with time across physical, cosmological, and philosophical scales.
By activating the animation, the participant enters a meditative state of presence within a landscape of mechanical and material decay.
Interaction: Please gently wind the handle to activate the animation.
